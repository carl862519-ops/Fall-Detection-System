=== FALL DETECTION SYSTEM - COMPONENTS LIST ===

1. Arduino Uno R3 (or compatible)
2. Tilt Switch/Mercury Switch
3. Active Buzzer
4. RGB LED (or separate Red + Green LEDs)
5. Resistors: 2x 220Ω, 1x 10kΩ
6. Breadboard
7. Jumper wires
8. USB cable for power

Total Cost: ~$15 USD
Source: Local electronic shops in Mozambique
Purpose: Affordable fall detection for elderly care
